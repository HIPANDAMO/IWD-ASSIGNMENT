Hotels

There’s a lot of variety when it comes to Queenstown, New Zealand’s hotel and resort accommodation. Ranging from boutique hotels to internationally recognised resorts, many offer lake and alpine views of Queenstown's scenery.
Many large hotels and resorts are located centrally within walking distance of the town's centre, and if you’re after something away from the hustle and bustle, hotels line the shores of Lake Wakatipu and can also be found in neighbouring Arrowtown.With excellent facilities including restaurants that feature winelists rich with Central Otago vintages as well as spa, pool and sauna facilities, Queenstown’s hotel and resort accommodation is second to none.To make sure this is the case we have rigorous systems in place to set the standards and ensure our staff maintain them. Beechwood Lake Views

Welcome to Beechwood Lake View Apartment in Queenstown. We invite you to enjoy this modern split level apartment featuring breathtaking mountain and lake views. When you’re at home, take advantage of the self-contained kitchen and dining room located on the entry level.

Hilton Queenstown

Hilton Queenstown Resort & Spa boasts a unique position on the scenic blue shores of Lake Wakatipu, amid a majestic mountain backdrop. Venture out to explore the area's natural attractions, or go to the center of Queenstown to visit its authentic restaurants.

Heritage Queenstown

The vistas from Heritage Queenstown are the stuff of postcards. Soak in the amazing view of the towering Remarkables mountain range, rising from the rippling waters of Lake Wakatipu, from the comfort of your room.

Park Boutique Hotel

Queenstown Park Boutique Hotel is the perfect base from which to enjoy your holiday and explore the Southern Lakes region, one of the most diverse areas in the world. Here you will find some of the must-do scenic drives and short day trips in the South Island.
