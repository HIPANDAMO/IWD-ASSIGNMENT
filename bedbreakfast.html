Bed & Breakfast

New Zealand is well known for its warm welcoming hospitality, and there’s no better way to experience this by staying at one of Queenstown’s bed and breakfasts, homestays, farm stays, or hosted boutique lodges.If you are looking for accommodation with the personal touch, and the comforts of home, a hosted property is for you. Alpine Suites Bed & Breakfast

Situated just 5 km from the Airport and 3 Km from downtown Queenstown, Alpine Suites is truly located at the heart of the worlds adventure capital and New Zealand's number one resort destination - Queenstown.

The Arrow Private Hotel

The Arrow Private Hotel is a Luxury Boutique Hotel just five minutes walk from the centre of historic Arrowtown. Its contemporary design and attention to detail was acknowledged when the Hotel won a prestigious NZ Institute of Architects Award.

Glenorchy Lake House

Glenorchy Lake House is set near the lakefront in Glenorchy. 360 degree views and all day sun, as well as the proximity to the areas best natural attractions, make a holiday here a must. Guests rooms are designed for comfort with cotton linens, goose down duvets, electric blankets and plenty of top quality pillows.

Chalet Queenstown

Only 5 minutes walk from central Queenstown, Chalet Queenstown is a boutique Bed and Breakfast/Inn. With its superb views of Lake Wakatipu, the Remarkables and Queenstown Hill, Chalet Queenstown is the perfect location for your New Zealand holiday. Renown for it's perfectly appointed rooms.
